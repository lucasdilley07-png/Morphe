Who we are

Morphe ("the app," "we," "us") is an iOS fitness app published by [Developer / legal name]. Questions about this policy: [contact email].

What the app stores

Morphe stores the following locally on your device only, so the app can work and remember your progress between sessions:

  • Profile details you provide during setup — the name you choose to be called, plus your goals, sport and training-style preferences, experience level, available equipment, any injuries or limitations you enter, and display preferences.
  • Your training activity — workouts you log (exercises, sets, reps, weight, duration, notes), any in-progress session, and derived figures such as streaks and progress scores.

This information is saved in the app's private storage area on your device. It is not sent to us or anyone else.

What we do not collect

  • We do not operate any servers or backend, so your data is never uploaded.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or third-party SDKs.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites, and we use no advertising identifier.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone.

Device permissions

This version of Morphe requests no special device permissions. It does not access your camera, photos, microphone, location, contacts, or Apple Health.

How your information is used

Because the data never leaves your device, it is used for one purpose only: to run the app for you — showing your plan, tracking your workouts, and personalizing your experience.

Storage, security, and retention

Your information is stored in Morphe's private app container, protected by your device's built-in security and iOS app sandboxing. Your data stays on your device until you remove it. To delete everything Morphe has stored, delete (uninstall) the app. Doing so permanently removes all locally stored Morphe data.

Children's privacy

Morphe is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). Because the app collects no information from anyone and transmits nothing off the device, no personal data is gathered from children or any other user.

Purchases

This version of Morphe has no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or payments, and processes no payment information.

Future changes to the app

Features that would change data handling — such as accounts, cloud sync, coach–client messaging, or payments — are not part of this version. If a future version introduces them, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for your consent first.

Your rights and choices

All of your information lives only on your device and under your control. You can review it in the app and erase all of it at any time by uninstalling the app. We hold no copy of your data, so there is nothing for us to retrieve or delete on our side. Depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR or CCPA), you may have additional rights; since we neither hold nor process your data on any server, there is nothing for us to act on.
